Is mulch or rock better?Mulch often supports plant health and lower upfront cost; rock can reduce refresh frequency but needs fabric, edging, and plant/heat planning.
What matters most for paver patios?Base prep, compaction, drainage, edge restraint, paver selection, and freeze-thaw planning matter more than choosing by color alone.
What plants work best in clay soil?Plant choice depends on sun, drainage, mature size, maintenance level, and whether the bed needs soil improvement or drainage correction.
Can outdoor lighting be added later?Yes, but lighting is often cleaner and more efficient when planned with patios, walkways, retaining details, and landscape beds.
