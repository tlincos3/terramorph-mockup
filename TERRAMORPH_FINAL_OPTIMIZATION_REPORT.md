## Remaining weaknesses

1. **Account-side SEO/ads setup still needs confirmation.** Search Console submission, GA4/GTM conversion mapping, Meta Events Manager verification, and Jobber redirect behavior require account access or IDs.
2. **More real project proof would materially improve authority.** The site has project/photo proof, but the next leap is more named case studies with city, problem, solution, materials, and before/after photos.
3. **GBP/content loop is still external.** Google Business Profile needs ongoing photos, service updates, review requests, and project captions to reinforce the site.
4. **Competitor superiority is strong on structure/content, but local backlinks/citations are still the biggest off-site gap.** Site quality is ahead of many local landscapers, but authority will compound with citations, local PR, supplier links, chamber links, and partner mentions.
5. **True conversion tracking depends on Jobber behavior.** If Jobber cannot redirect to the thank-you page, completed form-submit attribution remains limited to outbound quote intent and phone/quick-form events.
